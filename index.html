About The Author

Chimamanda Ngozi Adichie

Chimamanda Ngozi Adichie is one of today's most inspiring writers. Born in Nigeria, she has captivated readers around the world with novels like Half of a Yellow Sun, Americanah, and Purple Hibiscus.

Her stories explore love, identity, and the search for belonging, while also shining a light on feminism and equality. Beyond her books, she is a powerful voice for gender equality and contemporary culture.

Adichie's works have received numerous awards and accolades, and her TED Talk "We Should All Be Feminists" has become a cultural touchstone, inspiring discussions about gender equality worldwide.
